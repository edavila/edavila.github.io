Eduardo Dávila

Assistant Professor of Economics
Yale University
eduardo.davila@yale.edu

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Yale University and a Faculty Research Fellow at the NBER.
I do research in Financial Economics and Macroeconomics, with an emphasis on normative questions.
I teach General Equilibrium and Welfare Economics, Financial Economics, and Normative Macro-Finance.
